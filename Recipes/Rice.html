Rice Recipe

Description

This is a Nigerian dish called 'Rice' and it can be cooked in different ways. Starting from Jollof rice to fried rice and white rice. Some even prefer coconut rice.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of rice
  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of oil

Steps

  1. Place the 4 cups of water on to heat and then rinse the 3 cups of rice in a bowl with water.
  2. Add the rinsed rice into the boiling water and add the salt and oil
  3. Allow to cook for 20 minutes
  4. Once cooked, pour into a sieve and allow the water to drain
  5. Serve and enjoy with your favourite stew!
